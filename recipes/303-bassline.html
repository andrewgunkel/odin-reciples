Description

A classic acid house staple recreated in Serum 2. Resonant, squelchy, and driven — perfect for raw techno, trance, or acid breaks.

Ingredients

  • Oscillator A: Saw wave
  • Oscillator B: Off (optional subtle square blend)
  • Filter: MG Low 24 with drive
  • Envelope 2 → Filter Cutoff
  • Distortion: Tube or Diode
  • Delay: Ping-Pong, short time
  • Reverb: Small room, low mix

Instructions

  1. Set Oscillator A to a saw wave, reduce unison to 1, and detune slightly.
  2. Engage the filter and route Envelope 2 to cutoff with a fast attack and short decay.
  3. Add resonance until it starts to “squelch” on each note.
  4. Drive the filter and apply a touch of Tube distortion for grit.
  5. Optionally, modulate cutoff with an LFO synced to tempo for rhythmic motion.
  6. Add subtle delay and reverb for space, but keep the mix dry and punchy.

Suggestions

  • Use Glide (Mono mode) for classic acid slides.
  • Automate Filter Cutoff and Resonance in your DAW for evolving phrases.
  • Layer with a sub oscillator for modern weight.
