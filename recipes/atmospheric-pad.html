Description

An ethereal evolving pad designed for ambient, trance, and cinematic textures. Smooth detuning, long envelopes, and lush modulation layers.

Ingredients

  • Oscillator A: Wavetable — “IcanhazKick” or “Analog BD_Sin”
  • Oscillator B: Slightly detuned saw
  • Unison: 7 voices (both oscillators)
  • Filter: MG Low 12 with light cutoff
  • Envelope 1: Long attack and release
  • FX: Chorus, Delay, Reverb, EQ

Instructions

  1. Blend Oscillator A and B for warmth — keep detune under 0.10 for stability.
  2. Set Attack to 3–4 seconds and Release to 5+ seconds for smooth fades.
  3. Low-pass filter around 6 kHz to remove harshness.
  4. Add Chorus for stereo width and subtle movement.
  5. Layer Reverb with long decay and low mix (20–30%).
  6. Optional: Map LFO 2 to Wavetable Position or Filter Cutoff for slow evolution.

Suggestions

  • Use macro controls to fade in brightness or reverb depth live.
  • Stack with a noise oscillator for soft air texture.
  • Great for ambient intros or breakdowns in trance and IDM.
