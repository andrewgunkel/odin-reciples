Description

A loud, rising tone straight from 90s warehouse culture. Use sparingly to announce breakdowns, drops, or existential awakenings.

Ingredients

  • Oscillator A: Sine wave
  • LFO 1 → Pitch (1 octave up)
  • Filter: High-pass 12 dB
  • Effect: Chorus, Delay, and Reverb
  • Optional: White noise from Noise oscillator

Instructions

  1. Start with a pure sine wave on Oscillator A.
  2. Assign LFO 1 to the pitch and set to ramp shape; rate 1–2 bars for smooth rise.
  3. Increase Filter cutoff with resonance to add tension as the pitch rises.
  4. Layer with white noise to simulate air pressure and widen the sound.
  5. Use Delay and Reverb for stereo space; automate Mix for intensity build-up.
  6. Render as audio to stretch or reverse for breakdown effects.

Suggestions

  • Automate LFO speed to make the siren accelerate or decelerate.
  • Pitch it down an octave for darker, techno-style sweeps.
  • Try FM from B for metallic resonance.
